About

Miku is a BOT for Whatsapp for economy, fun and moderation! Also count on other systems you have on Miku. The fishery is one of the main attractions of the bot! It's like an economy system that grounds itself in user activity. Your server members catch fish by being active in text and voice channels. Fish can be sold for coins.
